Resume Writing Tips

Creating a standout resume is key to making a great first impression. Here are some concise tips to help you craft a resume that effectively communicates your qualifications:

  1. Use Action Verbs: Start bullet points with strong verbs like "Managed," "Developed," or "Led."
  2. Quantify Achievements: Include numbers to highlight your impact (e.g., "Increased sales by 20%").
  3. Tailor Your Resume: Customize for each job by using keywords from the job description.
  4. Keep It Concise: Stick to one page, focusing on the most relevant details.
  5. Focus on Relevant Skills: Highlight skills directly related to the job, both hard and soft.
  6. Proofread Carefully: Avoid typos by reviewing multiple times; ask someone else to proofread too.
  7. Include Relevant Projects: Mention coursework or projects that demonstrate your skills.
