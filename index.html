Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why Futuros En STEM?

    FES is dedicated to preparing students for future STEM careers. Whether you're interested in science, technology, engineering, or any other STEM oriented careers, our programs provide the skills, networking and knowledge needed to prepare you for post college.
    ^

  • How do I Join FES?

    Start by following us on Instagram @futurosenstem. Our Instagram page includes updates, event announcements, and Collaborators from Futuros En STEM.
    ^

  • Need help getting an internship?

    FES provides guidance on resumes, interviews, and developing a portfolio to impress your future employers. We offer resources and workshops to help you present your skills and experiences effectively to potential future employers.
    ^

  • How can I get my resume reviewed?

    Stay informed about our mentorship sessions by following us on Instagram @futurosenstem. We regularly post updates, including dates and details about upcoming La Sala sessions. Mark your calendar, and don't miss the opportunity to get personalized feedback on your resume.
